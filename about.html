About D.J.

For the past 6 years, DJ has worked as a mechanic at Honda Peninsula Ducati Kawasaki KTM. He is both Honda and Ducati factory certified. DJ also worked as a mechanic for Grand Prix Kawasaki Suzuki Yamaha.

DJ is an avid rider as well, and participates in many local rides. When he's not turning a wrench, you can usually find him either out on the trail, or enjoying some of the great South Bay roads that are right in his back yard.

While visiting DJ Cycles, you may get a chance to meet the shop mascot, the dog.
